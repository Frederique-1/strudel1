Creamy apfel strudel

Apfel strudel is a traditional Viennes pastry that is mainly eaten in Austria, the Czech Republic and other contries that once belonged to the Austro Hungarian Empire.
It is a pastry made from mainly flour, oil or butter and apples. The oldest know strudel recipe is from 1697, but if you go to Eastern European contries you still have a big chance to find a caf√© where they serve them. It can be served with vanilla, wipped cream, custard or my favorite, vanilla sauce.
